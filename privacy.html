Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information submitted through this website and the linked property-intake form may be collected, used, and protected.

Information collected

Information may include your name, email address, telephone number, property address or parcel number, county, property details, and other information you voluntarily submit.

How information is used

Information may be used to review a property inquiry, communicate with you, evaluate a possible real estate transaction, maintain business records, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Text messaging and mobile information

If you voluntarily provide a mobile number and separately consent to text messaging through the REsimpli intake form, you may receive conversational and informational text messages regarding your property inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

Mobile information, SMS consent, and opt-in data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service providers

Information may be processed by service providers that support website hosting, communications, customer relationship management, security, or recordkeeping.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of your information, subject to applicable legal and recordkeeping requirements. You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP.

This template is general information and is not legal advice.
