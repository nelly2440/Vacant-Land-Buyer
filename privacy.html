Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vacant Land Buyer (“I,” “me,” or “my”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website.

Information collected

I may collect your name, email address, telephone number, property address or parcel number, county, property details, and any other information you voluntarily submit. Basic technical information may also be collected by the website host, such as browser type, device information, and IP address.

How information is used

Information may be used to review a property inquiry, communicate with you, respond to questions, evaluate a possible real estate transaction, maintain business records, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Text messaging and mobile information

When you voluntarily provide your mobile number and separately check the SMS consent box, you agree to receive conversational and informational text messages from Vacant Land Buyer concerning your property inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

Mobile information, SMS consent, and opt-in data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may be shared with service providers solely as necessary to support messaging operations, subject to confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

Consent and opt-out

SMS consent is optional and is not a condition of submitting a property inquiry or entering a transaction. You may revoke SMS consent at any time by replying STOP or by contacting me using the information below.

Service providers and transaction professionals

Information may be provided to service providers that support website hosting, communications, customer relationship management, security, or record keeping. If you choose to pursue a transaction, relevant property and contact information may also be provided to professionals involved in the transaction, such as a title company, closing agent, attorney, surveyor, or other service provider. Mobile opt-in information will not be shared with those parties for their independent marketing.

Data retention and security

I retain information only as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above and applicable legal or record keeping requirements. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information, subject to applicable legal and record keeping requirements. You may also ask not to receive future communications.

Children

This website is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13.

Policy changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.
